TLTBO Term 2


The second season of LTB has heaps to offer!

Contents

With the second term of Lunchtime Basketball just a few days away, I thought it would be best to breakdown any changes surrounding the tournament for the term. I will be covering three different topics, a) the draft b) the website c) the tournament. Enjoy!







The Draft

Just like how last term we had a draft, this term we have another. However, there are a few major changes. The first big change is the introduction of retaining players. Each GM now has the option to retain a player that they had on their team, with an exception that the 5 Musketeers can retain someone from the Wet Willies (a compensation to the fact that Michael Iffland, a former 5 Musketeer, is now the Wet Willies GM). Player retainment is still to be announced. The remaining 9 players will enter the draft, where the league will follow a snake format, each team getting a pick. As following the Term 1 Champions, the Wet Willies, demands, they will get the first pick in the draft, the 5 Musketeers the second, and the Loose Gooses third. The order then reverses for the second round, and starts again for the third round. The announcement of the draft is also different this term, as each pick will be announced on the Draft page. Each pick will be seperated by one minute, meaning the whole event will last ~9 minutes. Player retainment will also be announced before the draft starts. The time of the draft is TBD, but you can see the countdown on the page.







The Website

I'm sure by now you've noticed by now, but there have been a number of changes to the website. The most noticeable change is the colour scheme switch, with the website now having more orange, and the logo is now blue. These are simply small changes to make things stand out more. However, there has been another, much more major change to the website: the Stats page. Stats is a new, easy way to quickly view and compare individual stats. It tracks a range of categories: points, finishes, midranges, and threes, as well as any accolades and the history of the player (e.g. being drafted to a team or traded). It also features results from each day. Currently, the stats page has updated accolades, but random stats to highlight what it does. The new stats page will make it significantly easier to track and see your stats, rather than opening the excel file (however, this is still available in the Results page if you wish for more detailed results)







The Tournament

While there aren't a huge heaps of changes to the tournament, I'll go over some of the basic changes. Firstly, the structure of the tournament is as so: Week 1) Preseason Week 2-6) Regular Season Week 7-8) Finals Week / Grand Finale Week 9) Postseason. Secondly, there have been some slight rule changes, such a prohibiting trade requests, and the rule of '3 fouls and you're out' has been removed. However, the free throw rule still stands as usual.







And those are all the changes to the tournament for this term! If anything doesn't make sense, or you have more questions, feel free to contact me.
