Off/Def Top 5 Rankings | 10/02

Clarrie Jones has released the fourth Offensive and Defensive top 5 of the season.

Offensive and Defensive All-5

Offensive:

  1. Alexander Galt
  2. Lukas Johnston
  3. Rudy Hoschke
  4. Nicholas Szogi
  5. Micahel Iffland

Alex and Lukas both recorded 4 buckets each, and some dominate offensive play to cap off the spot at ½. Rudy had one less point then Nic, however his lack of turnovers and passing play gave him the 3rd spot.



Defensive:

  1. Rudy Hoschke
  2. Samuel McConaghy
  3. Michael Iffland
  4. Angus Walker
  5. Clarrie Jones

Rudy Hoschke dominant with 4 blocks, as well as some impressive lockdown defence on some of lunchtime basketballs biggest names. Samuel also played will, keeping good players to the outside, and limiting their shots. Michael Iffland recorded to blocks, and Angus had a ‘quiet’ day, which basically means he didn’t record 5 blocks and 5 steals. I recorded 4 steals.

The top 5's takes all manners of basketball into account. Any questions or complaints, email me.

- Clarrie Jones, guard of the Wet Willies
