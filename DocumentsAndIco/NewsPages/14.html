Trade | 09/02

Involving the 5 Musketeers and the Loose Gooses

BREAKING NEWS

In a blockbuster trade, Number 1 pick William Kim has been traded to the Loose Gooses, along with forward Sam James. In return, the Loose Gooses have given the 5 Musketeers 3rd pick Michael Iffland, well known for his recent start-up of 'Munkee's magic moment', as well as guard Jasper Collier, who is looking to solidify his home in the tournament. The news comes after an extreme dominance from the Wet Willies, (winning 8 games in a row), with both other teams going back to the drawing boards to find how they can win more games. GM of the 5 Musketeers, Samuel McConaghy, had this to say about the trade - 'Kimmy has been an instrumental part of our team and family, and we are sad to see him go. But we have moved on to bigger and better things. We believe Micheal Iffland will be a great addition to our team with his lockdown defence, and deadeye from the block, and could not be more happy with this trade. I also welcome Jasper to our team, who, while has been struggling as of late, I know can be deadly in the midrange.' On the other side of the trade, Loose Gooses GM Angus Walker also commented on the trade: 'I am incredibly excited to have him [William Kim] join our team. He's a straight scorer, and can lock up anyone in the league. It's such a shame I had to let go of so much talent in Michael and Jasper, but I believe Kimmy and Sam James are the missing piece to our success in this tournament. I wish nothing but the best for Michael and Jasper in the future.' With both teams making major changes to their lineup, the big question stands: Is it enough to dethrone the command of the Wet Willies? Only time will tell.
