Off/Def Top 5 Rankings | 23/02

Clarrie Jones has released the fourth Offensive and Defensive top 5 of the third week.

Offensive and Defensive All-5

Offensive:

  1. Rudy Hoschke
  2. Samuel McConaghy
  3. Alexander Galt
  4. Lukas Johnston
  5. Michael Iffland

It’s official. Rudy is him. 4 points on the day, with awe-inspiring spins, euro’s, and post fades to guide his wet willies to cement their place at number 1. Alex equalled Rudy in points, however his volume of shots meant he was not as much of a threat



Defensive:

  1. Rudy Hoschke
  2. Samuel McConaghy
  3. Michael Iffland
  4. Alexander Galt
  5. Angus Walker

A rare occurrence, the top 2 on each all team were the same. Despite the loose gooses shocking performance, Angus Walker sneaks into 5th spot, with some nice reads on defence to force turnovers.

The top 5's takes all manners of basketball into account. Any questions or complaints, email me.

- Clarrie Jones, guard of the Wet Willies
