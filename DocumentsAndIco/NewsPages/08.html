TLTBO All-Second Teams


The LTBO has released their All-Second Teams from the second season!

After two months of hard work, we have reached Finals Week. Over the tournament, every player has played their heart out, doing the most they can to give their team a chance to win the championship. After each day, Loose Gooses guard Clarrie Jones would release rankings for offence and defence, from 1 through to 5. A player was rewarded 5 points from coming first on the list, and 1 point for coming fifth. Each of the players on this list made the Top 5 at least twice in the season, which is an incredible accomplishment seeing the amount of skill in this league. Congratulations to everyone selected on these lists.



These, are your TLTBO, Season two,

Offensive and Defensive Second Teams




Offensive:

SG: Nicholas Szogi (7 points)
