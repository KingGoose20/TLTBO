Clarrie Jones has released the FINAL Offensive and Defensive top 5 of the season!

Offensive and Defensive All-5

All year, the players in the LTBTO have battled their hearts and souls to give their team the wins, the points, the glory, the pure bliss, of winning games at lunch. However, this is not all that people have battled for. After all this time, are you read for…………

THE FINAL OFFENSIVE AND DEFENSIVE ALL TEAMS!


Offensive:

PG: Angus Walker

SG: Samuel McConaghy

SF: Lukas Johnston

PF: William Kim

C: Rudy Hoschke



Defensive:

PG: Angus Walker

SG: Samuel McConaghy

SF: William Kim

PF: Rudy Hoschke

C: Alexander Galt



ANGUS WALKER RUDY HOSCHKE WILLIAM KIM AND SAMUEL MCCONAGHY TAKE A BOW!!!! These four outstanding gentleman have had their names in both of the all teams. Their outstanding play throughout the entire term was nothing short of orgasmic. Us lesser beings could do little more then sit on our asses and watch as these truly spectacular human beings played the sport of basketball with an elegance and grace that we could only hope to achieve.

Angus Walker brought his game to both ends enviously, with great defensive reads, impressive dimes, and incredibly tough shots. Rudy played a similar game, his size proving to be too much for everyone else. Samuel is a true runner of the point, his hand on the rudder of the ship that is the 5 Musketeers, steering them to every single win they achieved. William Kim, with a beauty and grace that only he could achieve, dropped in bucket after bucket, day after day. Truly outstanding by everyone. Lukas Johnston is a pure scorer, with incredibly tough shots made repeatedly, and done with outstanding athleticism. Alex Galt, with his size and power, was truly a force to be reckoned with, his size compared to all of ours could be likened to a gun at a knife fight.

Thank you all for participating. I will still be releasing the best offensive/defensive performances of the day, however these are the true all teams from the season. It has been a true pleasure.




The top 5's takes all manners of basketball into account. Any questions or complaints, email me.

- Clarrie Jones, guard of the Wet Willies
