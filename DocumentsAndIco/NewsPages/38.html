Off/Def Top 5 Rankings | 08/03

Clarrie Jones has released the third Offensive and Defensive top 5 of week 5.

Offensive and Defensive All-5

Offensive:

  1. Angus Walker
  2. William Kim
  3. Lukas Jonhston
  4. Alexander Galt
  5. Christopher Tomkinson

Angus had to quickly remind us not to forget about his basketball ability. Dude balled out with 4 day-high 4 points and did so with class. William Kim came in with three, and its safe to say his offensive production after a few slow days is back up and running. Lukas also had three and scored all three with an ease only he could get. Chris gets on the list with the third highest points and was a big factor in the Loose Gooses success.



Defensive:

  1. Angus Walker
  2. Lukas Johnston
  3. William Kim
  4. Alexander Galt
  5. Mitch Yue

Angus proves his value as an elite two-way player, cementing his name firmly in the TLTBO history books. Lukas Johnston somehow found a way to carry his 3 MAN TEAM to second place in todays play, which is incredibly impressive. Kimmy was great in the Loose Gooses dub, and Alex and Mitch did their things with interior and perimeter defence respectively.

The top 5's takes all manners of basketball into account. Any questions or complaints, email me.

- Clarrie Jones, guard of the Wet Willies
