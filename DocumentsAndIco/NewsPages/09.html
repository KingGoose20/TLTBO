TLTBO All Teams


The LTBO has released their All Teams from the second season!

After two months of hard work, we have reached Finals Week. Over the tournament, every player has played their heart out, doing the most they can to give their team a chance to win the championship. After each day, Loose Gooses guard Clarrie Jones would release rankings for offence and defence, from 1 through to 5. A player was rewarded 5 points from coming first on the list, and 1 point for coming fifth. These players are the 6 strongest, most talented players in the league. Congratulations to everyone selected on these lists.
