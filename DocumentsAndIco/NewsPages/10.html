BREAKING NEWS

Trade | 08/02

Involving the Wet Willies and the Loose Gooses

BREAKING NEWS

Playmaking guard Clarrie Jones of the Loose Gooses has been traded to the Wet Willies, for guard and forward Jasper Collier. The trade was finalised on the 8th of Feburary, two days after Clarrie Jones had requested a trade due to problems with teammates. Loose Gooses GM, Angus Walker, commented on the trade, saying "I really hate to see him [Clarrie] go. He had a really good impact on our team, always gave it 100%. I enjoyed our time together, but the best things must come to an end. I welcome Jasper to the Loose Gooses with open arms, and am excited to win with him." GM of the Wet Willies, Rudy Hoschke, also commented on the trade, saying that 'We believe that Clarrie will add a vital part to the team that he is coming onto through his play-making ability and perimiter defence.'. It will be interesting to see how this trade spans out for either team, especially seeing how the two guards typically guard each other.
