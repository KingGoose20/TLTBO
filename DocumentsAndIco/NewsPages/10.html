Rudy Hoschke wins the Term 2 Scoring Title!


Wet Willies star Rudy Hoschke is the recipient of the Term 2 TLTBO Scoring Title

Scoring a whopping THIRTY EIGHT points over the season, including a record 12 points in just one day, your All-Offence and Defence star, reigning champion, and TLTBO Manager, Rudy Hoschke, has won the TLTBO scoring title for the second season. With Finals approaching, the league will need to step up if they want to prevent this man from single-handedly taking over. We've seen him do it before, theres no doubt he can do it again.
