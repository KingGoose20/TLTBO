State of the Website


TLTBO CEO Angus Walker breaks down the changes that have occurred to the website over the term.

Hey everyone, it's your favourite CEO Angus Walker and today I'm going to break down the various changes that have happened to the website. Over this term I’ve been making lots of changes to the website, to make it as cool and epic for you guys as it can be, so I thought I should share the best ones with you.





1. Team animations on ladder page

Most of you have probably noticed by now, but whenever you hover over a team’s image on the ladder page, a funky animation will play, and will show the players on that team. After a few seconds the animation will go away, and you will be able to check out the other teams. Click HERE to check it out!





2. Results growing animation

Once again, you’ve probably noticed this by now, but whenever you hover over a players name under ‘box score’ on the results page, their name will grow to make their results easier to see. Additionally, the ‘game by game’ section will rotate through emphasising each game, so that everyone’s bucket and win gets shown off.





3. Team Records from the day

A smaller, but helpful change that I’ve added, is seeing each teams record from the most recent day played. On the results page, underneath where it says which team will start with the ball, every teams record (recorded as wins-losses), their win percentage from the day, and the number of points their team will receive are listed. Looking at this section is a good way to quickly see how each team performed from the day.





4. Random images on news pages

As you can see on this page, every time you load a news page a different NBA picture (8 in total) is shown. This small feature helps to make each page look more unique, and it’s always cool to see more action pictures.





5. See stats on results and ladder click

Finally, the last change that I’ll go into detail about is that on the ‘results’ and ‘ladder’ pages, whenever you see a players name, or their stats, you can click on it to automatically take you to their stats page. This can be helpful for when you see someone got a few buckets in a day, and you want to see how they’ve been playing over the season.






While these are probably the most helpful features, there are about some more that I've added that I won't go into detail about. Below is a list so that you can check any of them out if you'd like.

  1. News Articles grow when hovered on
  2. Results and Stats lists' have alternating background colour
  3. Headings have colour animations
  4. Heading links grow when hovered on
  5. Calendar page has dates when hovering on
  6. Everyone's Stats picture has team-coloured border
  7. The third Sam (super cool edition) - can you find him?




Finally, I want to thank you all for using my website lots. We are already at 1700 total views, which is one-third of how many we got last term - pretty good seeing we've only played 4 days this season. If you ever have any feedback or ideas for the website, email me because I always want to make the website bigger and better.

- Angus Walker, TLTBO CEO
