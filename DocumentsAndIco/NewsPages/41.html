Special Awards

The first ever LTBO 'special awards' have been announced! There are 5 awards, each voted for by the various players in the league. The awards are:

  1. The Team Player Award: Awarded to the player who has the best sportsmanship, is always looking out for their teammates, and making sure everyone is having a great time.

  2. The 3rd Man Award: Awarded to the best player in the league who is not the first or second 'option' on their team.

  3. The Playmaker Award: Awarded to the player who creates the most shots, both for themselves and for their teammates, and knows how to play the game well.

  4. The Perimeter Defender Award: Awarded to the player who can guard anyone to the highest level outside or around the three point line (typically specialising in steals and intense pressure).

  5. The Most Improved Player Award: Awarded to the player who has imporved the most over the course of the season.

The LTBO sends a huge congratulations to all the special award winners. Each winner deserved their award, and there couldn't be a better pick than them. Willie, you have always been the upbeat, fun teammate that everyone loves to be around, so there's no surprise that you won the award. Michael, winning two awards is incredible, and both were deserved. Your playmaking and scoring ability makes a huge impact on your team. Ryan, you've always been a top tier perimeter defender, able to lock up anyone and get steals. And Chris, going from not being drafted to averaging 0.8 points (8th place in the league), and helping to bring your team to first is an outstanding accomplishment.
Best of luck to you all throughout finals week, make sure you continue to show why you won those awards!
