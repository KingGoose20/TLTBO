Munkee's Magic Moment | 10/02

Lukas Johnston's cool banana's shot is MMM

G'day everyone, and welcome to the second instalment of the almighty Munkee's Magic Moment! After receiving hate from the other (worse) award creator, William John Weekes, my opinion has remained unchanged, in that Willie's POTD is irrelevant, despite it being introduced before the start of the season. All people are talking about now is Munkee's Magic Moment and how cool and sexy and handsome the creator of it is. Anyway, beef aside, here's your second ever Munkee's Magic Moment: The 5 Musketeers with the ball after losing their last match up. Having missed every shot he'd taken so far, Lukas Hammond Johnston gets the ball at the right block, guarded closely. "Ain't no way he hits this," I thought to myself. All of a sudden, Lukas decides to shoot a fadeaway over the defenders!! "Damn, what a silly little boy," I thought. As the ball flies toward the basket, the whole team's eyes light up with amazement, no-one doubting the brilliance in execution from Lukas. The ball softly floats into the basket, winning the game for The 5 Musketeers!!! This moment, whilst sealing the win, also gave The 5 Musketeers the confidence boost and motivation they needed to win their next couple matchups!!
