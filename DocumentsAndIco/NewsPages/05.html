The Tournament Starts Today! | 6/02

Learn everything to prepare yourself.

Welcome!

Due to a recent turn of events, the Official Lunch Time Basketball Tournament will start today (the 6th of Feburary). The tournament will run until the 15th of March (Wednesday Week 6). Finals week will be from the 13th of March to the 15th.
MVP's and All-Teams will be announced every Friday, with the final results being announced on the 10th of March.
Rules: Please navigate to the 'Rules & FAQ' page to see the Official Tournament Rules. Some may surprise you!
Due to their preseason success, the Wet Willies will verse the 5 Musketeers in the first game of the tournament! Check-off will be at 1:27pm.
Stats and results will be uploaded after every break. While no one likes stad padding, try to up your stats and prove yourself!
Willie's Play of the Day: Wet Willies Forward William Weekes will be doing a Play of the day, which you can find on the News page. Can you do the best play?
