Willie's Play of the Day | 8/02

Chris Tomkinson's clutch 3 pointer is the PotD

It has recently come to my understanding that there is some competition in the LTBL highlights industry. Some NPC named Michael Iffland has decided to start his own daily section where he reports on the “most pivotal moment or clutch over the day.” B-O-R-I-N-G (Yawn emoji). I believe that the Willie POTD army should rise up and defend the OG daily reviews page against this rip off!!!! Enough banter for now, back to what you’ve all been waiting for: Willies day 3 review (Oh YEah) Chris Tomkinson, the all-time great of the G-League, tosses and turns in his bed. He is up all night, nervous about his debut match for the Loose Gooses. When he rocks up to warm up before the match, the Loose Gooses mock him (especially Michael). “G-league scum” they call him, “Your ginormous muscles won’t be any good in this league” they continue. Chris decides he’s had enough and walks, defeated, to the exit door. But just then, Angus Walker the GM extends a hand. Shocked, Chris shakes it and they get back to business. Later that day, the Loose Gooses are getting their asses absolutely handed to them by the all powerful Wet Willies. Chris Tomkinson receives the ball outside of the 3 point line. Surely not, nuh uh, not gonna happen, no POTD for Chris, surely this G-League grub will air ball it ha ha. But NO, the ball banks off the backboard and goes in (Holy Dooly). Congratulations Chris, you are the Willie’s Play of the Day winner for Day 3. Cya next time for some more Yeeeeeew - Tastic moments.

- Willie Weekes, forward of the Wet Willies
