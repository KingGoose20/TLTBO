Munkee's Magic Moment | 23/02

Japser Collier's tough elbow shot is MMM!

G’day chyamps!!! I know it’s been a long time coming, but welcome back to Munkee’s Magic Moment! After being switched up to a weekly award, it is finally time to announce the MMM for the week!! As you may have noticed, Willie’s Play of the Day has become quite slack when it comes to performing to a deadline, with the last POTD being long forgotten. This just goes to show the vast difference in effort and quality between Willie’s POTD and MMM, and this Willie character has the nerve to come at MMM for being a so-called, “rip-off”. Anyway, its time to announce the MMM of the week!!!!! The Loose Gooses are on a stellar run, winning the first 5 games of the day! Both the Wet Willies and the 5 Musketeers had a little bit of brown substance running down the backs of their legs. At long last, it was time for this player to show his worth. To rise up above all odds, and defeat the Loose Gooses. The 5 Musketeers were on the court, theirs heads hanging low, but they still had some faith left. The 5 Musketeers get the ball, and pass it to Jasper at the right elbow. Every player on and off the court turned their heads towards him, as he jumped in the air, and shot the ball towards the hoop. Everyone was in awe as the ball flew through the air. It did not look like it was going to be a swish, however there was something else. A tingly feeling that arose in everyone’s testicles. The ball smashes against the backboard, and… SWISHES OFF THE BACKBOARD!!!!!!!! This magic moment led the 5 Musketeers to winning the next 3 of their games, a true pivotal moment in the game. That’s all folks, join me next week for another Munkee’s Magic Moment!

- Munkee Man, forward of the 5 Musketeers
