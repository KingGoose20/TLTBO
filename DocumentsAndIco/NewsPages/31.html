Breaking News | Dethroning!

Behind Samuel McConaghy's two points, the 5 Musketeers have dethroned the Wet Willies!

Just out now

Due to having one less loss than the Wet Willies, the 5 Musketeers have dethroned the Wet Willies for the first time in the entire tournament. This is great news for the league, as the Wet Willies have long been thought to be unbeatable, but today had proven that belief false. The 5 Musketeers GM, Samuel McConaghy, showed out today, scoring 2 of the teams 3 points. Despite a slow start, he has started to pick up the pace, averaging 2 points over the last 2 weeks. Each position on the ladder is now only seperated by 1 point, meaning that it is truly anyones game.
