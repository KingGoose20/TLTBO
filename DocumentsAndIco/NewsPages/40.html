Off/Def Top 5 Rankings | 09/03

Clarrie Jones has released the last Offensive and Defensive top 5 of the regular season!

Offensive and Defensive All-5

Offensive:

  1. William Kim
  2. Clarrie Jones
  3. Angus Walker
  4. Christopher Tomkinson
  5. Samuel McConaghy

William Kim pulled up with a tournament high 6 POINTS!! I had the second most points on the day, scoring all three of the Wet Willies points on a day without our two best players, Rudy and Alex, giving us the second-place spot. Angus had equal third most points, with some nice passes and plays to go with it.



Defensive:

  1. Angus Walker
  2. William Kim
  3. Samuel McConaghy
  4. Christopher Tomkinson
  5. The Wet Willies

Angus Walker was a lockdown today, playing a massive part in his team only losing 3 times. Much is the same for William Kim. Chris earns his first defensive list, with some nice shutdown plays. However, our first ever all team tie! With the very undermanned Wet Willies managing not to finish last, their temporary big three played well defensively to come in with a 3 way tie at 5th!

The top 5's takes all manners of basketball into account. Any questions or complaints, email me.

- Clarrie Jones, guard of the Wet Willies
