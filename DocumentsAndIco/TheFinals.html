The Finals


From the 29th of May to the 9th of June

THE LOOSE GOOSES ARE THE LTBO CHAMPIONS!





After taking down the 5 Musketeers 7-3, the Loose Gooses, led by Angus Walker, are the new TLTBO Champions!
