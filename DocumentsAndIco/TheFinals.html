Which team has what it takes to win the LTB tournament?







The Grand Finale of the Inaugural Lunch Time Basketball Organisation Tournament will take place on Friday, the 24th of March. It will start at approximately 8:30am, and will run until 10:15am. The timetable is as so:


  • 00:00am-8:20am: Prepare yourself for the grand finale
  • 8:20am-8:30am: Player introductions, final warmup
  • 8:30am: The finale starts, with the Wet Willies (1st) facing off against the Loose Gooses (3rd)
  • 8:30am-9:00am: Part 1 of the Grand Finale
  • 9:00am-9:05am: 5 Minute break
  • 9:05am-9:35am: Part 2 of the Grand Finale
  • 9:35am-9:40am: 5 Minute break
  • 9:40am-10:15am: Part 3 of the Grand Finale (more on this below)
  • 10:15am-11:30am: Hungry Jacks for food
  • 11:30am-12:00pm: Return to school, award presentation and speeches

The first two parts of the Grand Finale last for approximately 30 minutes, ending once a game is finished over the 30 minute mark (we do not stop a game half way through). The break gives players time to rest, talk over what they need to do, and for the score keepers to process and share the results. As the whole Grand Finale will have 90 minutes of playing time, players are encouraged to use the rest breaks to avoid exhaustion. However, the third part is slightly different. We will play the average number of games in each part (e.g., if 12 games were played in part 1, and 10 in part 2, we play 11 games), rounding up if required.

If at the end of all the games, the difference between first and second is less than 1.5 points (1 point, 0.5 points, or 0 points), the two teams will play a first-to-7 (not a best-of-7) to determine the tournament winner. (The same applies for second/third to make the final game)

Once the tournament is over, the group will head down to Hungry Jack’s for some post-tournament food. Please make sure you have a spare change of clothes for this.

After Hungry Jack’s, the team will return to the courts, where each player will be recognised for any accomplishments that they have made, and anyone who would like to make a speech can do so. This will end the tournament for Term 1.
