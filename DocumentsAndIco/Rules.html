FAQ

  • Why does no one want to trade for Mitch Yue? (guard of the Loose Gooses)
    Because he lacks the right attitude and motivation to build team chemistry


  • Why did Clarrie Jones force his way out of the Loose Gooses?
    Apart from the secretive stuff going on, Clarrie thinks he is too good for the Loose Gooses.


  • Why is Sam McConaghy a bad GM?
    Because he cannot be bothered to email his team, even if it would help them improve.


  • When does the tournament end?
    Finals week starts in week 8 of school, with the tournament finishing at the start of shutdown (to be confirmed). League MVP will also be announced at the start of the finals week.


  • What is the point of an MVP?
    A league MVP is a fun way for the players to decide who had the biggest impact for their respective team over the course of the season. While it has no physical benefits, it is an accolade that will be shown for the rest of your LTBO career.
