FAQ

  • What is the LTBO?
    The Lunch Time Basketball Organisation is a highly competitive basketball competition that runs throughout the school term, restarting each term. It is a way for players to have a fun, competitive, time with friends that encourages getting better and striving to win


  • How long does the tournament last for?
    The preseason will run for the first week, acting as a way for the new teams to learn how to play together. After that, the regular season will run until the week before shutdown, which will mark the start of finals week.


  • What is finals week?
    Finals week is the last week of TLTBO tournament before the grand finale. It is more competitive, with higher stakes and more ladder points available
